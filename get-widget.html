📥 Download Shortcut File
(May show "Untrusted" warning on iPhone)

✨ Recommended: 60-Second Setup

1Open Shortcuts app
Find the blue "Shortcuts" app on your iPhone.
2Tap + icon
Top right corner → Create new shortcut.
3Add "URL" action
In bottom search bar, type "URL"
Tap the blue "URL" result.

Paste this URL: (tap to copy)
https://gustavoibenc.github.io/intermezzos/intermezzos.json
4Add "Dictionary" action
Search "Dictionary" → Tap "Get Dictionary Value"

In Key field, type: (tap to copy)
delivered[0].fact
5Add "Show Result"
Search "Show" → Tap "Show Result"
6Name & Add to Home
Tap ⋯ (three dots) at top
Name: Intermezzo Fact
Tap Share icon (square with arrow)
Scroll → Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Tap Add

✨ DONE! Widget on your home screen!
💡 Pro Tip: Long-press the widget after adding to customize its icon color!
